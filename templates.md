## Category 1: Cold Outreach (10 Templates)

### 1.1 — The Value-First Opener
**Subject:** Quick idea for {{company_name}}
**Body:**
Hi {{first_name}},

I noticed {{specific_observation_about_their_business}}. I had an idea that could help:

{{one_sentence_value_proposition}}

I put together a quick 2-minute breakdown — want me to send it over?

Best,
{{your_name}}

**Why it works:** Leads with value, not a pitch. The observation shows research. Low commitment ask.
